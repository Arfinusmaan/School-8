Welcome to QB365 Public School

Established in 2000, QB365 Public School has been a beacon of educational excellence for over two decades. We are committed to providing quality education that nurtures young minds and develops future leaders.

Our school offers a comprehensive CBSE curriculum from Nursery to Class XII, supported by state-of-the-art infrastructure and experienced faculty. We believe in holistic development that combines academic excellence with character building, sports, and cultural activities.

At QB365, every student is encouraged to discover their unique talents and pursue their passions while developing strong moral values and leadership skills.
